# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard focuses on analyzing workforce data to uncover insights related to employee demographics, compensation, and attrition. It helps organizations better understand employee trends and supports data-driven HR decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

* To monitor overall workforce statistics
* To analyze employee attrition patterns
* To understand the impact of age, education, and salary on retention
* To identify trends across job roles and tenure
* To build an interactive and insightful HR dashboard

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following key indicators:

* **Total Employees**
* **Attrition Rate**
* **Average Time Period (Tenure)**

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of workforce health and stability.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes a variety of visualizations to explore HR data:

* **Attrition by Years at Company**
  Shows how employee turnover varies with tenure.

* **Attrition by Job Roles**
  Identifies roles with higher attrition rates.

* **Employee Distribution by Age**
  Provides insight into workforce age groups.

* **Employee Distribution by Education**
  Highlights educational backgrounds of employees.

* **Salary Analysis**
  Displays salary trends and distribution across employees.

## 🧰 Tools & Techniques Used

* KPI cards for quick insights

## 🎨 Dashboard Elements

* **KPI Cards** for key HR metrics

## 🔍 Insights Gained

* Identification of departments and roles with high attrition
* Understanding of how tenure impacts employee retention
* Correlation between salary and attrition trends
* Workforce distribution across age and education levels
